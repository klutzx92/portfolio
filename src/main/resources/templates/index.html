My journey as a software engineer started in 2019 when I transferred to UC Berkeley and it has been the ride of my life. Following the advice of my community college CS professor (thank you Prof. Darwiche!), I changed my major from Psychology to Computer Science in my first semester and grinded like never before in order to declare the major. As a transfer, I was determined to make the most of my education with the limited amount of time afforded to me. I explored my passion for the subject, excelling in courses such as Data Structures, Efficient Algorithms, Database Systems, and Operating Systems -- all while interning at an early stage AR startup and contributing to an open-source application for the EECS department. Graduating last May (during a pandemic no less) was the greatest achievement of my life, fulfilling the generational hopes and dreams of my immigrant parents and becoming the first in my family to obtain a college degree. Since graduating, I have been fortunate enough to work at Tata Consultancy Services where I have the opportunity to learn and develop more skills such as developing full-stack web applications using Java Spring Boot and developing test automation software using Selenium and the POM design pattern.

My journey through it all has taught me to embrace change, fail fast, and face each challenge with equal parts determination and positivity. Above all, I gained an immense passion for growth, learning, and working collaboratively with others. Please feel free to reach out to me if you think I would be a great fit for any opportunities and thank you for learning about me!
